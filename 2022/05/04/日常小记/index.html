Canary栈保护

https://developer.arm.com/documentation/101754/0616/armclang-Reference/armclang-Command-line-Options/-fstack-protector---fstack-protector-all---fstack-protector-strong---fno-stack-protector#:~:text=%2Dfstack%2Dprotector%2Dall%20adds,of%20any%20size%20and%20type.

-fstack-protector-all adds stack protection to all functions regardless of their vulnerability.

-fstack-protector-strong enables stack protection for vulnerable functions that contain:

  • An array of any size and type.
  • A call to alloca().
  • A local variable that has its address taken.
